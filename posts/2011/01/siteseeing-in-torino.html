Siteseeing in Torino

Posted: 2011-01-17 00:00   |  More posts about Travel Jterm11   |   Source

Today was a tourist day, we started with a tour of the Egypt Museum, and then split up to wander the city.  Craig and I visited the church where the shroud of Turin is kept, had lunch at a Mongolian BBQ restaurant, visited the Powerstation exhibit at the modern art museum, walked around the Fiat test track and enjoyed the sites of the mountains, and walked by the Olympic stadium.   Here are some photos that tell the story.
